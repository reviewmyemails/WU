About

The person behind Review My Emails.

Yanna-Torry Aspraki

Founder, Review My Emails

I started Review My Emails because I kept seeing the same problem. Marketers would run their lists through validation tools, get a clean bill of health, and then watch their deliverability tank anyway.

The tools were not lying. They just were not telling the whole story.

Validation checks if an address exists. It does not check if sending there is safe. It does not know about complaint histories, engagement patterns, or domain-level risk signals that accumulate over time.

I spent years in email deliverability, working with senders of all sizes. I learned that list health is not binary. An address can be technically valid and still damage your reputation. The question is not "will this bounce?" but "should I send here?"

That is what Review My Emails answers. Every address gets labeled Keep, Monitor, or Suppress. And every label comes with the reasoning behind it. No black boxes. No guessing.

If you have questions about your list or your deliverability, I am happy to talk. That is what the SOS Hotline is for.
