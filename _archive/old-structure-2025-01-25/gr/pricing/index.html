What You Get

A cleaned list with clear recommendations.

Keep

Cleared After Full Review

No risk patterns surfaced during review. Domain behavior and historical signals support continued sending.

Monitor

Patterns Worth Watching

These require judgment. We surface historical patterns and relationship context. You decide how to segment based on your tolerance and goals.

Suppress

Clear Risk, Clear Reasoning

Clear risk signals: known problem domains, disposable patterns, historical trap indicators. Each address includes the specific flags that earned suppression. You will know exactly why.

Every decision comes with the reasoning behind it. This is not a pass/fail list. It is a decision-making tool built on historical domain behavior and pattern recognition.

Who This Is For

Marketers who already send email and want to protect their sender reputation.

  • Running campaigns but seeing declining engagement
  • Worried about bounces, complaints, or blocklists
  • Inheriting a list you did not build
  • Under pressure to "just send" but sensing risk
  • Trying to explain deliverability to a boss who does not get it

If you care about long-term reputation, not just short-term volume, this is for you.

The Problem

Most list cleaning tools give you a status and leave you guessing.

Deliverable. Undeliverable. Risky. Unknown.

That tells you what to remove. It does not tell you what is actually wrong.

  • Why is engagement dropping?
  • Why are complaints rising?
  • Is it the list, the sending, or both?
  • Which addresses are actually hurting me?
  • What patterns should I watch for?
  • What should I do about the "risky" ones?

We give you the context to answer those questions.

What You Are Actually Paying For

The difference between checking and interpreting.

  • Validators check if an inbox exists. We assess whether sending there is safe.
  • Validators give you a status. We give you the reasoning behind a decision.
  • Validators flag what bounces. We flag what damages your reputation without bouncing.
  • Validators process volume. We interpret patterns across your entire list.
  • Validators leave you guessing. We tell you what to do and why.
