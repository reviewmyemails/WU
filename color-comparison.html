Background Color Options

Comparing alternatives to the mint background for section contrast

Option Colors Used Pros Cons
A: Neutral Gray #f8fafc #f1f5f9 Clean, professional, universally readable Less brand personality
B: Subtle Gradient Gradient from #f8fafc to #e2e8f0 Adds depth, modern feel Can look dated if overdone
C: White + Borders White with #e2e8f0 borders Maximum contrast, very clean Less visual separation
D: Current Mint #ccfbf1 #e0f7f4 On-brand teal family, warm Visibility concerns
E: Warm Gray #faf9f7 #f5f3f0 Warmer feel, approachable Less "tech" aesthetic
F: Teal-Tinted Gray #e6f7f3 #d1f0e8 #c2ebe0 On-brand + readable Slightly less "neutral"
G: Teal Gradient Gradient from #f8fdfb to #d1f0e8 Brand personality + depth More visually active
