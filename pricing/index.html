The Problem

Most list cleaning tools give you a status and leave you guessing.

Deliverable. Undeliverable. Risky. Unknown.

That tells you what to remove. It does not tell you what is actually wrong.

  • Why is engagement dropping?
  • Why are complaints rising?
  • Is it the list, the sending, or both?

We give you the context to answer those questions.

What You Are Actually Paying For

The difference between checking and interpreting.

  • Validators check if an inbox exists. We assess whether sending there is safe.
  • Validators give you a status. We give you the reasoning behind a decision.
  • Validators flag what bounces. We flag what damages your reputation without bouncing.
  • Validators process volume. We interpret patterns across your entire list.
  • Validators leave you guessing. We tell you what to do and why.
