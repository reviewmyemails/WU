Which feels right?

Both maintain professionalism. The question is how much visual personality to add.

A: Hint of Waves

  • Subtle curved section dividers
  • Light texture in footer only
  • Sea theme mainly in copy
  • Clean, minimal, lets content lead

B: Consistent Sea Presence

  • More pronounced wave edges
  • Dot/line textures in sections
  • Depth gradients in dark areas
  • Unmistakably "ocean" brand
