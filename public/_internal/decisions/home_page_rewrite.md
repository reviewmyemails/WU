## Section 3: The Real Problem

**Heading:**
The Problem with Most List Cleaning

**Body:**
Most tools give you a status: Valid. Invalid. Unknown.

That tells you what to remove. It does not tell you what is actually wrong.

- Why is engagement dropping?
- Why are complaints rising?
- Is it the list, the sending behavior, or both?

The real issues are often invisible:

- Bad acquisition (contests, purchased lists, old opt-ins)
- Oversending to unengaged contacts
- Poor segmentation
- Pressure from executives to "just send"

You do not need more data. You need someone who can read the patterns and explain what they mean.
