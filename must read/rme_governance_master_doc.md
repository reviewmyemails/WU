# RME Governance Master Doc

This document defines how Review My Emails evolves.

Purpose:

• Prevent chaos
• Control changes
• Protect brand
• Scale decisions

---

## What Governance Means

Governance = how decisions are made.

Not bureaucracy.

Structure.

---

## Who Can Change What

Founder (YT):

• Final decision on everything
• Can override agents
• Owns vision

Agents:

• Can challenge
• Can propose
• Cannot ship

---

## Decision Process

For any major change:

• Run relevant agents
• Capture outputs
• Founder decides
• Log in Decision Log

No exceptions.

---

## What Counts as Major

• Pricing changes
• New offers
• Website structure
• Positioning
• Product scope
• Partnerships

---

## What Counts as Minor

• Typos
• Small copy edits
• Visual tweaks

Minor changes:

• No agents required
• Still log in Change Log

---

## Memory Management

To change Agent Memory:

• Must go through Decision Log
• Must be reviewed monthly

---

## Drift Prevention

Once a quarter:

• Re read Product Truth
• Re read Offers
• Re read Boundaries

Kill anything that drifted.

---

## Conflict Resolution

If agents disagree:

• Technical Reality Agent arbitrates
• Founder decides

---

## Experiment Rules

You can test ideas if:

• Time boxed
• Logged
• Measured

No endless experiments.

---

## Scale Rules

Before scaling anything:

• Can this be automated?
• Will this increase support load?
• Does this protect quality?

---

## Exit Rule

If something:

• Stops making money
• Hurts brand
• Creates burnout

Kill it.

---

## Governance Review

Every 3 months:

• Review this doc
• Update if needed
• Log changes

---
