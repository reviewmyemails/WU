# RME Objection Library

This document captures every reason people hesitate to buy or book.

And how you respond.

This feeds:

• Website copy
• Sales calls
• Agents
• FAQs

---

## How to Use

Whenever you hear an objection:

• Log it here
• Write the real words they used
• Add your best response
• Note which proof supports it

---

## 1. "We already use a validation tool"

What they mean:
They trust software.

Your response:

• Tools catch syntax.
• They don’t catch patterns.
• They don’t explain why things happen.
• We help you understand risk before it costs you.

Proof to reference:

• Julia S review
• Chris Mead review

## 9. "Is this 100% accurate?"

What they mean:
They want guarantees.

Your response:

• We validate every address individually.
• Those checks are real.
• We also help you understand what impacts inbox placement.

• Results are shared responsibility.

What we promise:

• Honest analysis
• Risk reduction
• Clear guidance

What we don’t promise:

• Inbox guarantees
• Perfect accuracy
• Magic fixes
