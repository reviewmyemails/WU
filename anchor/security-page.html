Security FAQ

Common questions about how we protect your data.

Who has access to my email list?

Only Yanna-Torry Aspraki — the founder and sole reviewer. There are no employees, contractors, or third-party services with access to your data. Your list is reviewed on a single encrypted workstation that is not connected to cloud services.

How long do you keep my files?

All files are permanently deleted within 48 hours of completing your review. If you need your files deleted sooner, just email us and we'll delete them immediately with written confirmation. No backups or archives are ever maintained.

Is my data used to train AI models?

Absolutely not. Your data is never uploaded to any AI service (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) and is never used for machine learning training. All analysis is done manually by a human expert on a local, offline workstation.

What encryption do you use?

We use AES 256-bit encryption at rest and TLS 1.2+ for all data in transit. File uploads go through Dropbox File Requests, which provides the same encryption standards used by major financial institutions. Learn more about Dropbox security →

Can I request immediate deletion of my data?

Yes. Email support@reviewmyemails.com at any time to request immediate deletion. You'll receive written confirmation once your files have been permanently removed — typically within hours.

Where is my data stored?

Uploaded files are temporarily stored in encrypted Dropbox storage (US-based infrastructure with SOC 2 Type II certification). During review, files exist only on Yanna-Torry's local encrypted workstation. No data is stored on third-party servers beyond Dropbox's secure file transfer.
