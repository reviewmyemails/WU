Recording Guidelines

Quick setup guide for a professional recording

PRIORITY #1

Audio Comes First

If you have to choose between better audio or better video, always choose audio.

External mic
Laptop mic
Wired headphones
Bluetooth (can lag)
Quiet room
Echo/background noise

Video

  • Camera at eye level
  • Stable setup (no handheld)
  • Landscape orientation
  • Head & shoulders in frame

Lighting

  • Face the light source
  • Light from front, not behind
  • Window light works great
  • No harsh overhead lights

Background

  • Neutral, uncluttered
  • Quiet, private space
  • Virtual BG OK if clean
  • No visual distractions

Internet

  • Stable connection
  • Wired if possible
  • Close other apps

What to Wear

  • Professional & comfortable
  • Solid colors
  • No tight patterns
  • No shiny fabrics

Day Of

  • Arrive on time
  • Test setup beforehand
  • Have water nearby
